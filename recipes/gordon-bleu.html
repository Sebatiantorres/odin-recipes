How to Make Freezer-Friendly Chicken Cordon Bleu

This recipe allows you to prepare Chicken Cordon Bleu ahead of time and freeze it for a quick and easy meal later. Perfect for busy weeknights!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 1 inch thick)
  • 4 slices ham
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (panko or regular)
  • 1/4 cup melted butter or oil

Directions

  1. Butterfly each chicken breast.
  2. Fill with ham and cheese.
  3. Combine flour, salt, and pepper.
  4. Beat the egg.
  5. Place breadcrumbs in a dish.
  6. Bread the chicken.
  7. **To Freeze:** Place breaded chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and freeze.
